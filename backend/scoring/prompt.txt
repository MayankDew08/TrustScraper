═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TRUST SCORE FORMULA (for your reference)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Trust Score = (
    w1 × author_credibility   [weight: 0.30] +
    w2 × citation_count       [weight: 0.20] +
    w3 × domain_authority     [weight: 0.20] +
    w4 × recency              [weight: 0.15] +
    w5 × medical_disclaimer   [weight: 0.15]
) × abuse_multiplier

All component scores are in range [0.0, 1.0]
Final score is in range [0.0, 1.0]
Weights sum to exactly 1.0

COMPONENT SCORING RULES:

author_credibility:
  INSTITUTIONAL tier (Harvard, Nature, NIH, WHO):
    - Starts from institution floor score (0.80-0.95)
    - Boosted by individual credentials found in name/bio
    - NOT penalized for missing followers or claps
    - Rationale: institution carries credibility, not social metrics

  OPEN PLATFORM tier (Medium, YouTube, blogs):
    - Starts from 0.25 base
    - Built up by: credentials, followers, bio, citations
    - Penalized for: anonymous name, no credentials, no bio

  PUBMED:
    - Author count drives score (peer review signal)
    - 5+ authors → 0.95, 3-4 → 0.90, 2 → 0.85, 1 → 0.80

citation_count:
  INSTITUTIONAL: neutral 0.60 (editorial process implies quality)
  BLOG: log10(clap_count + 1) / 4.0
  PUBMED: log10(citation_count + 1) / 2.0
  YOUTUBE: log10(view_count + 1) / 6.0

domain_authority:
  INSTITUTIONAL: hardcoded tier score (0.80-1.00)
  OPEN PLATFORM: base 0.45 + author credibility boost (max ceiling 0.82)
  YOUTUBE: base 0.52 + channel boost (max ceiling 0.78)

recency:
  0-90 days    → 1.00
  91-180 days  → 0.90
  181-365 days → 0.80
  1-2 years    → 0.60
  2-3 years    → 0.40
  3-5 years    → 0.25
  5+ years     → 0.10
  Unknown      → 0.40

medical_disclaimer:
  INSTITUTIONAL: 0.85 (implied) or 1.00 (explicit)
  Non-medical content: 0.50 (neutral, not applicable)
  Medical + disclaimer: 1.00
  Medical + no disclaimer + advice: 0.00
  Medical + personal essay (no advice): 0.40

abuse_multiplier:
  1.00 = clean (no abuse signals)
  0.70-0.99 = mild signals (keyword stuffing, suspicious patterns)
  0.30-0.69 = severe signals (fake author, misinformation phrases)
  Floor at 0.30 (never zeroed completely)
